🎳 How to Win at Skee-Ball

Skee-Ball may look simple, but there’s real finesse behind those high scores. These tips will help you roll like a pro:

  • Go for the 40-point hole — it’s safer and more consistent than chasing the 100s in the corners.
  • Stay low and bend your knees — this gives you better control and a smoother release.
  • Use your whole arm — think bowling, not flicking. A straight roll is key.
  • Find your release point — aim to let go when the ball is level with your chest or chin.
  • Practice makes perfect — muscle memory and rhythm are everything in Skee-Ball.

Bonus tip: Some pros aim for the back of the pocket to increase their chances of hitting the 100-point hole.
