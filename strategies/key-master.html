🗝️ How to Win on Key Master

This video shows how Key Master machines are designed to payout only after a certain number of fails. The trick is knowing when it’s ready—watch for the prize alignments and use light taps for control.

Tips:

  • Don’t play unless you’ve seen 10+ fails.
  • Use short, soft taps to move the key into position.
  • If the key “should” have fit but didn’t, it’s not ready to payout.
